KEY LARGO FISHING CHARTER

Looking for your next big fishing adventure? There is no place on earth for that like Key Largo. The Florida Keys are not only known for the world’s best fishing grounds but the ability to accommodate fishing year round. Whether it’s reef or offshore fishing, there is no better fishing charter than Key Largo Fishing Adventures.

At Key Largo Fishing Adventures we aim to please. If this is your first or 50th charter fishing excursion, we want it to be one you’ll be talking about for years. We offer a fun and relaxed but professional atmosphere from the novice to experienced angler. We welcome children, families, small groups and business travelers alike. We pride ourselves in delivering 100% satisfaction to our guests on every trip and we’ve been doing that for over 20 years. Check out our Facebook, Google or Trip Advisor reviews to see what our guests are saying about us.

Conveniently located in Key Largo in the Florida Keys, we’re just an hour south of Miami, one and half hours from Ft. Lauderdale and two hours north of Key West! It’s a short drive for an amazing experience to the place considered the fishing capital of the world. There are so many species of fish available here depending on the season.

Deep Sea and offshore we catch dolphin (mahi-mahi), tuna, wahoo, sailfish, marlin and Kingfish.

Reef and wreck fishing we catch grouper, snapper, hogfish, amberjack, cobia, Sharks, mackerel and barracuda.

We offer excellent discounted room rates for the following hotels: Bayside Inn, Holiday Inn Key Largo, Marriott Courtyard Key Largo, Marina Del Mar Key Largo, Marriott Key Largo Bay Resort, Key West Inn (Key Largo). We also offer the opportunity for split charters or shared charters. Please call to for more hotel rate info or split and shared fishing charters info.

Call or email us today to come aboard with one of our professional captains and enjoy a true Florida Keys fishing charter. Let us be your guide to a day on the beautiful crystal blue waters of Key Largo and experience the thrill of the catch.
